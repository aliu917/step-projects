Part 4 of Winter 2019 Australia Trip

Trip Planning/Notes

  • Hotel: Pullman Melbourne on Swanston - newly remodeled and very convenient location, although staying anywhere in CBD is incredibly convenient and walking distance (or free tram distance) to all Melbourne city attractions

  • Rent a car for one day to visit the Great Ocean Road

  • Check out the free tram services here (file from Public Transport Victoria)


Trip Diary

  • Day 1 - Travel Half Day

    • Hopetoun Tea Rooms

      Got off the plane, checked into our hotel (Pullman Melbourne on Swanston) and took a quick walk to The Block Arcade for an afternoon snack. Happened to stop by right when they were closing so we took some cakes to go.

      • If you have the option, eat in since they give extra sides (like ice cream)

      • Favorite: Apple Peach Blackberry Crumble

      • Also tried: Bakewell tart, passionfruit tart (was half melted though since we ate it the next day), Apple Cinammon Walnut Tea Cake, scones with jam

    • Free Tram Ride

      Got on at the State Library of Victoria stop and hopped off at Federation Square.

    • Dragon Hot Pot

      Spotted this popular hot pot restaurant near our hotel and enjoyed a large hearty meal. Buffet style entrance where you pick all raw ingredients you want (meats, vegetables, noodles, etc), pay by weight, and then they cook it up for you and serve it back in one LARGE bowl.

  • Day 2 - Great Ocean Road

    • Shrine of Remembrance

      Strolled through the Royal Botanical Gardens but most of it wasn't open yet (as we were there before 8 am)

    • Kettle Black

      Super cute and classy cafe with quite large and filling brunch options.

      • Favorite: Seasonal local mushroom toast

      • Also tried: chilli scarmbled eggs with crab, avocado toast, and poached eggs

      • Grabbed some pastries to go for the Great Ocean Road!
